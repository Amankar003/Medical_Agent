=========================================
DIETARY ADVICE AND NUTRITIONAL HEALTH
=========================================

1. Diet for Hypertension (High Blood Pressure):
The DASH (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension) diet is highly recommended globally.
- Consume plenty of vegetables, fruits, and whole grains.
- Include fat-free or low-fat dairy products, fish, poultry, beans, and nuts.
- Limit foods that are high in saturated fats, such as fatty meats and full-fat dairy products.
- STRICTLY limit your sodium consumption to no more than 1,500 mg to 2,300 mg per day.

2. Diet for Diabetes (Blood Sugar Management):
- Prioritize complex carbohydrates that have a low Glycemic Index (GI), such as oats, brown rice, and lentils, instead of white rice or refined flours.
- Ensure every meal has a good source of lean protein and dietary fiber to prevent sudden spikes in blood sugar.
- Avoid sugary beverages, fruit juices, and processed snacks. 

3. Diet for Acid Reflux:
- Foods to Eat: Oatmeal (absorbs stomach acid), non-citrus fruits like melons or bananas, lean poultry, and green vegetables.
- Foods to Avoid: Tomatoes, onions, garlic, coffee, chocolate, peppermint, and deep-fried foods.

4. General Immunity Boosting:
- Ensure adequate Vitamin C intake through citrus fruits, bell peppers, and broccoli.
- Zinc is critical for immune function; find it in seeds, nuts, and legumes.
- Drink warm turmeric milk or ginger tea, which have natural anti-inflammatory properties.
